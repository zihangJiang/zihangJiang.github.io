Hi, I'm Zihang Jiang, a research associate with the School of Biomedical Engineering at the University of Science and Technology of China, Suzhou Institute for Advanced Research. I received my Ph.D. degree from National University of Singapore, ECE department advised by Prof. Xinchao Wang and Dr. Jiashi Feng. I received my Bachelor degree in Computational Mathematics from Hua Loo-keng Talent Program of USTC in 2019, advised by Prof. Juyong Zhang.
